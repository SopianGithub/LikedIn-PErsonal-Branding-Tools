Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

LinkedIn Personal Branding Tool ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • LinkedIn Access Tokens: OAuth tokens necessary to post content to your LinkedIn account
  • Post Content: Draft posts, scheduled posts, and published content
  • Account Information: Basic LinkedIn profile information (name, profile picture) for display purposes
  • Usage Data: Analytics on post performance and engagement metrics
  • Content Library: Articles, papers, and content you upload or scrape

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Post Management: Create, schedule, and publish posts to your LinkedIn account
  • Content Processing: Analyze and process content for post creation
  • Analytics: Track post performance and provide insights
  • Service Functionality: Enable core features of the application

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in our database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Secure OAuth token management
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit consent

6. LinkedIn API Integration

Our application uses LinkedIn's API to:

  • Authenticate your account via OAuth 2.0
  • Publish posts on your behalf
  • Retrieve engagement analytics

We only access and use LinkedIn data that you explicitly authorize. You can revoke access at any time through your LinkedIn account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke LinkedIn API access
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (may limit functionality)

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
